# Billing FAQ

## When am I charged?

You're charged on the same day each month based on your signup date.

Example: If you signed up on October 15th, you'll be charged on the 15th of every month.

## How do I update my payment method?

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Payment Methods"
3. Click "Add Payment Method"
4. Enter your card details
5. Set as default if desired

Accepted payment methods:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Debit cards
- PayPal (Enterprise plans only)

## Where can I find my invoices?

1. Go to Settings → Billing → Invoices
2. You'll see all past invoices
3. Click any invoice to download PDF
4. Invoices are also emailed automatically

Invoice details include:
- Billing period
- Amount charged
- Payment method used
- Itemized breakdown

## How do I cancel my subscription?

We're sad to see you go! To cancel:

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Select cancellation reason (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation

After canceling:
- Access continues until end of billing period
- No prorated refunds
- Data retained for 30 days
- Can reactivate anytime

## Can I get a refund?

Refund policy:
- Full refund if canceled within 14 days of first payment
- No refunds for renewals
- No partial refunds for mid-cycle cancellations
- Exception: Service outages >24 hours

To request refund:
- Contact support@yourcompany.com
- Include order number
- Response within 2 business days

## How do I upgrade/downgrade?

Upgrading:
1. Go to Settings → Billing → Change Plan
2. Select new plan
3. Pay prorated difference
4. Upgrade takes effect immediately

Downgrading:
1. Go to Settings → Billing → Change Plan
2. Select lower plan
3. Change takes effect next billing cycle
4. No refund for current period

Plan comparison:
- Free: 100 API requests/hour, community support
- Pro: 1,000 requests/hour, email support, advanced features
- Enterprise: 10,000 requests/hour, phone support, SLA, custom contracts
